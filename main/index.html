About this system

This system is in place to help provide the SENCO of a school with easier access to information regarding students with disabilities.


The relevant information regarding the students in the school is stored and can be access through this site. There is also a range of useful reports that will be available for the SENCO to view in order to support their work.


This system stores information like student details (e.g. name), and their indiviual SEN and EHCP.
